umit scans the hosts in your network discovering open ports and the services and operating systems that they are running.


With umit, you have all the power provided by nmap through its  regular command line interface, and a lot more, in a highly usable and portable graphical interface.


Usage:


Scan your LAN and find out which hosts are up/down, ports, services offered by each host and their operating system info(Linux/Windows/etc).
