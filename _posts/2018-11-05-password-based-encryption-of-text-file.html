Encrypt:
openssl des3  -out ~/Personal_Data/PWD.x -in ~/Personal_Data/PWD.txt

Will ask for secret/password


Decrypt:
openssl des3 -d -in ~/Personal_Data/PWD.x -out ~/Personal_Data/PWD.txt

Will ask for secret/password
