 "Do Not Populate" means exactly what it says - do not put a resistor there.

It is typical for design work to include extra components that are used only during
development. They are discarded in the final product.

for example:
Resistor R667 in below schematic is marked as DNP, meaning do not put a resistor here. So DAT3 is not actually pulled down by this resistor (as this resistor does not exist in final hardware and it was only there in hardware development process).
