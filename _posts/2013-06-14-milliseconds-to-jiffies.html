If we want to convert 200ms into jiffies 

then

jiffies = 200 * (HZ / 1000);  or 

Jiffies = (200/1000) * HZ;

jiffies = (1/5) * HZ;

one fifth of HZ.

because 200ms = one fifth of a second.


so 1000ms = 1second = 1HZ jiffies.
