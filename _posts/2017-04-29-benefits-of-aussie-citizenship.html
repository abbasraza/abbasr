  1. Hassle-free travel and re-entry:A permanent resident can stay in the country indefinitely. However, if you want to go overseas and return to the country, you will need to apply for a Resident Return Visa every five years.
  2. Excellent consular support while overseas
  3. Federal government and defence jobs
  4. Visa free travel to 169 countries
  5. Financial assistance for education
