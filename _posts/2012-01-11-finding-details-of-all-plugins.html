Use following sequence of commands to get details of all the plugins supported by Gstreamer on your system. Details mean gst-inspect of all plugins. 


gst-inspect > supported 


vim supported


%s/\:  /\:  gst-inspect /
%s/\:  /\r/
%s/\:/\r/
wq




grep -Ii gst-inspect supported &> supportedInspectCMD


chmod +x supportedInspectCMD 


./supportedInspectCMD > supportedInspect
