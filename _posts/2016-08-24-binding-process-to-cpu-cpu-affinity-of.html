ubuntu@auwgtrun02:~$ taskset -p 14726
pid 14726's current affinity mask: 1           -----------> running on CPU1

ubuntu@auwgtrun02:~$ taskset -p f 14726
pid 14726's current affinity mask: 1
pid 14726's new affinity mask: f     -----------> will run on CPU1, CPU2, CPU3, CPU4 now
ubuntu@auwgtrun02:~$ taskset -p 14726
pid 14726's current affinity mask: f
ubuntu@auwgtrun02:~$
ubuntu@auwgtrun02:~$ taskset -p 1 14726
pid 14726's current affinity mask: f
pid 14726's new affinity mask: 1   -----------> will run on CPU1 now
ubuntu@auwgtrun02:~$ taskset -p 14726
pid 14726's current affinity mask: 1
