220v, 10(20)A, 50Hz, 600(Disk Revolutions)/kWh


Example:
Calculate the power being consumed in the house at present if the meter takes 2 min 15 sec (135 seconds) to rotate through 50 divisions, using a meter with a rev/kWh value of 133.3, and total number of divisions per revolution of 100.
P = (3600 * 50/100) / (135 * 133.3) = 0.1 KW or 100 Watts.
