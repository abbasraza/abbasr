TASK_INTERRUPTIBLE:
process sleeps until some condition becomes true like interrupt/signal/releasing of resource

TASK_UINTERRUPTIBLE:
same as TASK_INTERRUPTIBLE except wake on signal.

signal can be SIGKILL, SIGABORT (crtl-c?)
