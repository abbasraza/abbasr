  1. # of cores:
    i3 is dual core
    i5 is dual/quad core
    i7 is quad/6/7 core
  2. HT (Hyper Threading)
    i3 YES
    i5 dual YES, quad NO
    i7 YES
  3. Turbo Boost:
    i3 NO
    i5 YES
    i7 YES
  4. L3 Cache:
    i3 3MB
    i5 3 to 6MB
    i7 4 to 8MB
  5. CPU Clock speed:
    i7 > i5

So i7 is better than i5 because of 
  • hyper threading
  • turbo boost with higher clocks
  • big L3.
