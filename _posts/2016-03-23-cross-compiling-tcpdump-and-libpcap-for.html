download tcpdump and libpcap from http://www.tcpdump.org/

use following commands to compile:

1) libpcap
2) tcpdump


CC=arm-linux-gnueabi-gcc ./configure --host=arm-linux-gnueabi --with-pcap=linux --prefix=$PWD/installed/
