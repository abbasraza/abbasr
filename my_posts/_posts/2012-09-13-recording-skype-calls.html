I was in need to record some skype calls and then i came accross the Skype Call Recorder

Just download "skype-call-recorder-ubuntu_0.8_i386.deb" package and install it on your host system.
It will appear in the Applications =>Accessories in the startup panel on Ubuntu. When you launch it, it will connect to the Skype running. And whenever you make a call, it will ask you for recording and then will save the recorded calls in /home/abbas/Skype Calls directory. 
Enjoy !
