Default Speed card: 
root@mx6q:~# cat /sys/kernel/debug/mmc1/ios
clock:          25000000 Hz
vdd:            17 (2.9 ~ 3.0 V)
bus mode:       2 (push-pull)
chip select:    0 (don't care)
power mode:     2 (on)
bus width:      2 (4 bits)
timing spec:    0 (legacy)
