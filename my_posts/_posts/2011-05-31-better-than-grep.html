ack-grep is a utility in linux which is like grep.. but is said to be better than grep as you can narrow down your search results easily by using its features.
Currently i am using it and it looks very cool .

Usage:

apt-get install ack-grep
cd sources
ack-grep

and it will search your string in only .c and .h files.


More info can be found in ack-grep help or here.
