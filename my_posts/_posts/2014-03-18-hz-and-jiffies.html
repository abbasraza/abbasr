CONFIG_HZ=100 means 100 ticks in 1 second
Jiffy = tick count

so if HZ=100 then in 1 second jiffies count will be = 100 (i.e 100 ticks)

now 1 jiffy means how much time?
it means 1/100th of a second.

1/100th of a seconds = .01 second = 10ms.

so 1 jiffy = 10ms time.
    2 jiffies = 20ms time.
    100 jiffies = 1 second time.
