I was trying to connect my powerpc board via serial port to my ubuntu machine but it was not working at all. I was not able to connect to my board via serial port instead of all correct serial port settings on minicom i.e 8N1 . I thought that the serial port of my ubuntu machine was having some problem as i tried to connect my board with other machine and it was working. After googling i found the exact problem which was stopping communication to happen

So whenever working with serial port remember following steps in ubuntu


  1. Configure serial devices
    /bin/setserial /dev/ttyS0 baud_base 115200 auto_irq skip_test autoconfig
  2. Enable RTS/CTS handshaking
    stty crtscts -F /dev/ttyS0
 It solved my problem. ;)
