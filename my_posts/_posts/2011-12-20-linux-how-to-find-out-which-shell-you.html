I always do forget it :P

Here are three quick ways to find out which shell you are currently using:


Method 1:
command:
# echo $SHELL
output:
/bin/bash
Method 2:
command:
# echo $0
output:
-bash
Method 3:
command:
# ps -p $$
output:
 PID TTY               TIME CMD
1447 tty1          00:00:01 bash
