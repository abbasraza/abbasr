your computer[146.108.214.26] <---> NAT <----> Test Computer[any local ip] [username:ubuntu]

you want to ssh Test Computer from your computer?



1) On Test Computer:
================
sshpass -v -p 'password_here' ssh -o StrictHostKeyChecking=no -R 19999:localhost:22 abbasraza@146.108.214.26

2) On your computer:
================

ssh localhost -p 19999 -l ubuntu
